City Guide - Krakow

§A Beer & Bars in Krakow
A.1 Multi Qulti (multitap bar) - Old Town (Stare Miasto) - ~50.063, 19.941 - Many rotating taps; lively evenings.
A.2 Weźże Krafta (craft beer bar) - Kazimierz - ~50.051, 19.944 - Polish craft selection; relaxed vibe.
A.3 House of Beer (multitap bar) - Old Town (Stare Miasto) - ~50.061, 19.938 - Wide tap list; central and busy.

§B Lunch in Krakow
B.1 Hala Forum - Podgórze - ~50.046, 19.948 - Many stalls; perfect for mixed groups and bad weather.
B.2 Hummus Amamamma - Kazimierz - ~50.050, 19.943 - Casual vegetarian bites near the square.
B.3 Bar Mleczny Tomasza - Old Town (Stare Miasto) - ~50.062, 19.938 - Classic milk bar; famous for pierogi.

§C Sights & Attractions in Krakow
C.1 Main Market Square (Rynek Główny) - Old Town (Stare Miasto) - ~50.062, 19.937 - Largest medieval square in Europe; great for a stroll.
C.2 Wawel Royal Castle - Wawel Hill - ~50.054, 19.935 - Historic castle and cathedral above the Vistula.
C.3 Schindler's Factory Museum - Podgórze - ~50.047, 19.961 - Krakow under the Nazi occupation; book ahead.
